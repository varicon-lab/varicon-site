Why this model works

Traditional tools charge for:

  • seats
  • hosts
  • log volume
  • infrastructure

VARICON charges for understanding.

That means:

  • small teams can adopt it almost for free
  • larger teams pay when the product becomes mission-critical
  • pricing aligns with value, not setup friction

What counts as usage?

A paid unit is an enriched anomaly.

That means:

  • local detection is free
  • report generation is free
  • artifact generation is free
  • only calls into the intelligence layer are billed

Pricing FAQ

What is free?
Local detection, fingerprints, report generation, and CI integration are free.
What is paid?
Paid usage begins when anomalies are sent to the intelligence layer for classification, prioritization, or deeper analysis.
Do you charge per seat?
No. VARICON pricing is usage-based, not seat-based.
Can I run VARICON entirely locally?
Yes. The open-core layer can run locally without paid enrichment.
Can pricing be customized?
Yes. Enterprise plans can include committed volume, custom rules, and private deployments.
